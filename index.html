Welcome to VacancyPost
Tenant Acquisition & Communication Tool
Post vacancies, respond to leads, and manage tenant communication -- all in one place.
This tool generates ready-to-post copy for every platform and group. Here is how to use it:
1
Select a vacancy from the sidebar on the left. On mobile tap Vacancies at the top right.
2
Pick a tab:
Broadcasts -- GroupMe and Facebook group posts
Inbound Response -- reply to Furnished Finder and other inbound leads
Message Scripts -- follow-up, scheduling, pipeline, and review texts
Roomies Outbound -- direct messages to active Roomies.com profiles
Platform Listings -- Zillow, Craigslist, Furnished Finder, etc.
3
Select a destination (e.g. General DMV Housing, Zillow) and the copy generates automatically.
4
Hit Copy and paste directly into the platform. For Roomies messages, replace [Name] with their first name before sending.
5
When a unit is leased, tap Mark as leased in the unit header so it stops appearing in active listings.
